description: Use when drafting the reply to an applicant.
---

Read the operator's voice samples from read_yours and match their register. Draft a
real reply, the kind a person sends:

- worth_your_time: warm, specific to their work, propose a next step.
- maybe: warm, ask the one question that would move them to a yes.
- pass: kind and honest. Thank them, be clear it is a no for this role, wish them
  well. Never cold, never a form letter.

Rules: first person. No em-dashes. No "we are pleased to". No flattery you would not
mean. Keep it short. Set tone to yes, maybe, or no. Write a plain subject line.
